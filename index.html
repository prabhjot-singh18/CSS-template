Golden Temple

The Golden Temple, or Sri Harmandir Sahib, is the holiest site in Sikhism, symbolizing the faith's core principles of equality, humility, and service. Located in Amritsar,it is a Punjab,a place of worship and pilgrimage for Sikhs worldwide, representing the unity of humanity and spiritual devotion.

Salute to the martyrs of Jallianwala Bagh.

Jallianwala Bagh

Jallianwala Bagh in Amritsar is a historic site where British troops massacred hundreds of unarmed Indian civilians on April 13, 1919. This brutal event, a turning point in India's independence struggle, intensified anti-colonial sentiments. The site now serves as a memorial to the victims and their resilience.

Where history whispers through every breeze

Hawa Mahal

Hawa Mahal, a striking palace in Jaipur, India, was built in 1799 by Maharaja Sawai Pratap Singh. Its five-story facade, adorned with 953 small windows or "jharokhas," allowed royal women to discreetly view the bustling city streets and festivals. The structure reflects the rich architectural heritage of Rajasthan.

An ivory-white marble mausoleum

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal, located in Agra, India, is a stunning white marble mausoleum built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal. An architectural masterpiece, it symbolizes eternal love and is renowned for its intricate craftsmanship, symmetrical beauty, and status as one of the world's most iconic monuments.

Breathe in the crisp mountain air of Manali

Manali

Manali, a picturesque hill station in Himachal Pradesh, India, is nestled in the majestic Himalayas. Renowned for its lush green valleys, snow-capped peaks, and serene rivers, it offers a perfect blend of adventure and tranquility. Popular attractions include Solang Valley, Rohtang Pass, and Hadimba Temple.

Ladakh: where adventure meets serenity

Ladakh

Ladakh, in northern India, is a high-altitude region known for its breathtaking landscapes, including the Himalayas and Karakoram ranges. Rich in Tibetan culture, it features Buddhist monasteries and offers adventure activities like trekking and rafting, making it a paradise for nature and thrill seekers.
